Hi, my name is Nikki van Wijngaarden. I was born in Heiloo, but have lived in Alkmaar all my life. I'm a All around Game programmer and a Technical Artist. I find myself good at developing and programming gameplay, but I also really enjoy world building and 3D modelling. I myself am currently specializing in C# and would also like to learn C++ later.

As a child, I grew up with World of Warcraft and the Zelda games. When I was a little older I heard from my father that he had a World of Warcraft server and maintained it with good friends of his back in the day. That gave me the interest and inspiration to study game development. Later, together with my father, I made and programmed several 3D printers. Which gave me a little more insight into the robotics side of programming, which made me more aware that I actually enjoy developing games a lot more.

I was 15 when I started experimenting in Unreal 4 which was also the very first engine I started with, my idea was simple spell casting (healing) together with a health system and animations together with a small and simple inventory system. Unfortunately, I don't have that project anymore because I had never knew of github back then.

Furthermore, I am a fairly quiet person who can sometimes be a really hot head with a great passion for game development. Which I also sometimes express a little too much. I would also like to travel the world with friends and family.

I spend my free time a little part of it working and mostly playing games with my friends. My hobbies are drawing and gaming and spending time with friends and family.
